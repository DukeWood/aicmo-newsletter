The 5 Pillars of White-Hat GEO

The 5 pillars of white-hat GEO

1. Question-Driven Content

AI users ask questions, not keywords. Write 1500-3000 word comprehensive answers to real questions from Reddit, Quora, and support tickets.

2. Third-Party Citations

Being mentioned by others > self-promotion. Guest post, contribute expert quotes, participate in communities, publish original research.

3. Structured Data

Clarity is credibility. Add schema markup, use clear heading hierarchy, short paragraphs, and tables for comparisons.

4. Freshness

Recency drives relevance. Quarterly refreshes, new data, timestamps, and public changelogs show your content is alive.

5. Authority Signals

Trust is cumulative. Author bios with credentials, organization pages, cited studies, and guest experts build authority.
