## Design Guidelines

### Colors:
- **Crimson Red:** `#C8102E` (primary CTAs, headings)
- **Navy Blue:** `#012169` (secondary CTAs, subheadings)
- **Background:** `#f9f9f9` (light gray for footer)
- **White:** `#ffffff` (card backgrounds)
- **Text Gray:** `#666666` (body text)
- **Border:** `#E0E0E0` (dividers)

### Typography:
- **Headings:** 18-24px, bold
- **Body:** 14-16px, regular
- **Legal:** 12px, light

### Spacing:
- **Section padding:** 30px vertical
- **Card padding:** 30px all sides
- **Button padding:** 12-14px vertical, 24-28px horizontal
